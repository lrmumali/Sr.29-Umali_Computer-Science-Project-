Cardfight Vanguard

A trading card game that started out as a manga created by Author Akira Itou and eventually made its debut as a TCG back in 2011 with its first two trial decks, “Blaster Blade” and “Dragonic Overlord”, and its first booster set, “Descent of the King of Knights”, a few weeks later. Its mechanics can be said to be less complex and deep than its competitors, but it has been able to become one of the very few TCG’s to continue to stay alive even after years of releases, even going strong up until this day. Only earning less than the “Big 3” of trading card games: Yugioh, MTG and Pokemon. If you wish to learn more information, please refer to the links below.
